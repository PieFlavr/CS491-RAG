Find a Way into Wyrm's Rock Fortress

From bg3.wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Find a Way into Wyrm's Rock fortress is a quest in Act Three of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be started by approaching the Wyrm's Rock Fortress gate from Wyrm's Crossing.

Objectives[edit section | visual editor]

Find a way to get into Wyrm's Rock Fortress.
  • Wyrm's Rock is closed due to the ordination of Gortash - who is about to become Archduke of Baldur's Gate. There must be someone, or something, in Wyrm's Crossing that could help us gain entry.
Enter Wyrm's Rock Fortress through the secret entrance.
  • We found a map that details a secret entrance into Wyrm's Rock. That could be our way in.
Enter Wyrm's Rock Fortress.
  • We found a report for Duke Ravengard at the Posthouse. This should let us enter Wyrm's Rock.
  • We have an admission pass to the ordination. This should let us enter Wyrm's Rock.
  • Valeria gave us an admission pass to the ordination. This should let us enter Wyrm's Rock.
  • We convinced the guards to let us into Wyrm's Rock for Gortash's coronation.
Quest Complete
  • We made our way into Wyrm's Rock, where Gortash is about to become Archduke.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

There are a variety of ways to enter Wyrm's Rock Fortress. Only one of them needs to be pursued to gain entry to the fortress.

Getting in the Front Door[edit section | visual editor]

The party can walk in through the gate by speaking to  Blaze Elin at the gate at X: 6 Y: 155. Based on the party's actions before approaching the guards, they have the following dialogue options:

The following dialogue options offer a possibility to pass Elin's cordon without any obstacles:

  • See this? I've been invited to the ordination. (if the  Admission Pass was obtained)
  • As you can see, I have an urgent dispatch for Duke Ravengard. (if the  Urgent Report for Duke Ravengard was taken from the Sword Coast Couriers)
  • Investigator Valeria needs me in the Lower City - a serial killer's on the loose. (if the  Lower City Pass was given by Valeria)
  • Provide the pass you took from Valeria. (if the Lower City Pass was pick pocketed from Valeria)

Valeria's Pass[edit section | visual editor]

 Valeria is an inspector involved in the ⁠Solve the Open Hand Temple Murders quest. She is initially encountered in the Open Hand Temple X: -44 Y: -6. After this, Valeria can be found drinking upstairs in Sharess' Caress at X: 24 Y: 92. If finding enough information to exonerate  Brilgor of  Father Lorgan's murder, and finding the murder list in Fraygo's Flophouse, then Valeria refers the party to speak to  Devella Fountainhead at the Elfsong Tavern and provides a pass to the Lower City to allow them entrance.

Stealing an Admission Pass from Arfur Gregorio[edit section | visual editor]

 Arfur Gregorio, the Toymaker, carries an admission pass. He can first be encountered in front of Arfur's Mansion X: 11 Y: -95. If Arfur is allowed back in his mansion, then he is inside. If the refugees remain in the mansion, then he is on the first floor of Sharess' Caress at X: 21 Y: 75.

During ⁠Investigate the Suspicious Toys, Arfur can be confronted with the blackmail letter found in his mansion's basement. After entering the basement through the hatch X: 40 Y: -72, the blackmail letter can be found in a heavy chest at the southeastern corner of the room at X: 361 Y: -990. If Arfur is told I found some rather sinister things in your basement, followed up by I'll get to the bottom of this one way or another, Arfur becomes hostile and attacks the party. In this case, the pass can be looted off his body.

After finding the blackmail letter, the party can speak to  Manip Nestor at the Requisitioned Barn at X: 66 Y: -112 and persuade him to look into the barn for dangerous items. Arfur's explosive teddy bears can be found at the back of the barn X: 83 Y: -108. If speaking with Arfur after this, and telling him about his teddy bears, he can be intimidated into giving more information on his bosses. If he is spared after this, he gives the party his coronation pass, or the pass can be pickpocketed off Arfur.

Taking an Admission Pass from the Nymph's Grotto[edit section | visual editor]

An admission pass can be found inside Nymph's Grotto on the Upper Floor of Sharess' Caress. The pass is in front of the bath at X: 28 Y: 94. This pass presumably belonged to  Fist Jara before she turned into a Mind Flayer. Taking this pass does not count as stealing.

Duke Ravengard's Report[edit section | visual editor]

A report on Duke Ravengard can be found at Sword Coast Couriers X: 15 Y: 12 - on a cupboard behind  Danzo Arkwright. If this letter is stolen, it can be shown to the guards to get by.

Convincing the Guards[edit section | visual editor]

The party can attempt to convince the guards to let them pass by passing extraordinarily high  Persuasion or  Deception checks:

  • [PERSUASION] I'm here to protect Archduke-Elect Gortash. (DC 30)
  • [OATH OF THE CROWN] [PERSUASION] It is my sworn duty to protect the Archduke-Elect. (DC 30 )
  • [DECEPTION] An assassin is in pursuit of Duke Ravengard! I need to warn him! (DC 30)
  • [WARLOCK] [DECEPTION] I am Lord Gortash's new occultist - let me pass or your name goes in the Book of Vile Darkness. (DC 30)
  • [BARD] [DECEPTION] Please let me in - I'm meant to be in the coronation orchestra, it's my big break! (DC 30)
  • [PERSUASION] Such a bother. What if I were to contribute to the 'Flaming Fist's Orphans' Fund'? (DC 25) (Requires 20,000 Gold)
  • Not closed to Duke Ulder Ravengard's own son, surely? (if interacting as  Wyll)

Breaking into Wyrm's Rock[edit section | visual editor]

The party can forego the guards entirely by breaking into Wyrm's Rock. They can do this by jumping off the bridge using  Fly or  Feather Fall at the eastern side of the gate at X: 27 Y: 158. After this, walking eastward reveals a crack in the wall to the prison at X: 42 Y: 190 with a successful  Perception check.

After entering into Wyrm's Rock Prison, there is a wall that can be blasted open at X: 91 Y: -720 with blunt attacks or spells (e.g.  Eldritch Blast). This leads to a storage room. The door must be lockpicked open, as well as the door to the entrance at X: 52 Y: -740. There are multiple guards, that confront the party if they are spotted - requiring either successful sneaking or passing a deception check of 15.

Alternatively, it is possible to fly from Blaze Elin's side of the bridge to the other side, leading straight to the main corridor of the fortress.

Quest rewards[edit section | visual editor]

Entering Wyrm's Rock Fortress earns the following inspiration.

Using a pass:

  • ⁠Multipass ( Noble backgrounds) - only if using  Urgent Report for Duke Ravengard
  • ⁠Rock-Solid Alibi ( Charlatan backgrounds) - for using any paper

Without a pass:

  • ⁠Through Nooks and Crannies ( Urchin backgrounds)
  • ⁠Procedures be Damned ( Noble backgrounds) - this one includes an option to be arrested and come out into the fortress after escaping
