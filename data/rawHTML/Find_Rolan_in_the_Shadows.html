Find Rolan in the Shadows

From bg3.wiki

Find Rolan in the Shadows is a Quest in Act Two of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be started by speaking with the tiefling children at the Last Light Inn.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

If  Rolan was convinced to defend the Druid Grove in Act One and the Grove was defended during Save the Refugees, Rolan appears in the Last Light Inn. He is at the bar (X:-78, Y:148) drunkenly arguing with two tiefling children,  Umi and  Ide, who are refusing to give him more alcohol. If the party talks to him, he blames them for the loss of his siblings  Cal and  Lia, who were taken after a group of Absolute cultists ambushed the refugees. Rolan wanted to abandon the refugees and go their own way, but the party convinced him to stay and made Cal and Lia feel like heroes. He doesn't know if they're alive or dead, and he is determined to save them himself.

Regardless of if the party speaks to Rolan or not, he disappears after Resolve the Abduction. The party can find him at the southern end of the Ruined Battlefield and southeast of the Reithwin Tollhouse (X:-52 and Y: -110). When the party gets close to his location, he is attacked by several Shadows. If the party joins the battle and saves him, he admits he was trying to get to Moonrise Towers to see if his siblings were there. He is upset that the party had to rescue him, but agrees to return to the Inn, where he'll wait for his family to return.

Notes[edit section | visual editor]

  • If the party get too close to Rolan but either leaves or Long Rests, Rolan dies from the Shadows.
  • If the player character raided the Grove and told Rolan and his siblings not to stay, this quest is available but Rolan will not be found where the quest marker indicates he should be.
  • If the party rescues Cal and Lia before visiting Last Light Inn for the first time, Rolan is there to greet them, but still appears in the same location when the party visits it and acts as if his siblings were still imprisoned.
