Defeat the Duergar Intruders

From bg3.wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Defeat the Duergar Intruders is a Quest in Act One of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be initiated by approaching the Myconid Colony at Ebonlake Grotto peacefully, and speaking with  Sovereign Spaw.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

Ebonlake Grotto is in the northeastern corner of the Underdark, and Sovereign Spaw is on a mushroom platform in the heart of the Grotto at X: 78 Y: -102. As the party approach the Grotto, a group of myconids approach and confront them on why they are in the Underdark. There are several options to enter the Grotto peacefully:

  • Tell the truth of the parasite. You only seek safe passage in your search for treatment.
  • [PERSUASION] I am a traveller seeking adventure. (DC 5)
  • [INTIMIDATION] My purpose is private. Let me pass, and I will leave you unharmed. (DC 5)
  • [INSIGHT] Seek understanding. Focus on the sovereign's song. (DC 5)
    • I sense your fear. I've come to aid you.
  • [DROW HALF-ELF] [DROW] [DEEP GNOME] The Underdark is my home - I don't need your permission to pass.
  • [OATH OF THE ANCIENTS] I am nature's bulwark. Where good things grow, I lend my protection.
  • [OATH OF DEVOTION] To do what is right. To do my duty.
  • [OATH OF VENGEANCE] I seek retribution for those who cannot.
  • [OATH OF THE CROWN] I am a servant of the law in lawless places.
  • [OATHBREAKER] I have no 'purpose'. I simply am. That is enough.
  • [GITHYANKI] [BARD] Mumble an ancient gith axiom in rhythm with the sovereign.
  • [BARD] Hum a peaceful tune in harmony with the sovereign.
  • [BARBARIAN] [INTIMIDATION] ROAR. (DC 5 )

If the origin character is a Duergar or has one as a companion, it is harder to convince the sovereign not to become hostile, but it is not impossible:

  • [PERSUASION] Conjure tranquil memories in hopes of calming the sovereign. (DC 10)
  • [INTIMIDATION] If I wanted you dead, you would be. Don't tempt me. (DC 10)
  • [PERSUASION] Kneel in submission. I mean no harm to you. (DC 10)
  • [PERSUASION] Kneel in submission. My duergar companion means no harm you no harm. (DC 10)
  • [BARBARIAN] [INTIMIDATION] ROAR. (DC 10 )

Once things have been resolved peacefully with the sovereign, it explains that a group of Duergar slaughtered the myconids and greatly reduced their numbers. The duergar responsible are still lurking within the Underdark and the myconid circle is imperilled unless they are defeated. Spaw asks the party to deal with these duergar. Agreeing to help initiates the quest.

The duergar are in the Decrepit Village to the south-west at X: 11 Y: -195. If  Thulla is cured in ⁠Cure the Poisoned Gnome, she marks their location on the map. The duergars in question are led by  Gekh Coal, who instantly becomes hostile if the party recruited  Glut for ⁠Avenge Glut's Circle. Gekh Coal is joined by several duergar allies -  Lurgan,  Holvik, and  Novice Garmor. Several Reanimated Corpses are also controlled by the duergar.

Once the duergar are dealt with, the party must return to Spaw. It thanks the party and tells them how to get their reward; the cave behind the Guardian Gate at X: 56 Y: -89, which holds the treasure is north of Spaw's location. Most of the important items are on the pale corpse of  Xargrim, including a book needed for ⁠The Adamantine Forge.

Spaw names the party Peacebringer, and tells them that it has one more quest for them. There is a Drow called  Nere in Grymforge who has also hurt the myconids. Spaw wants his head brought to it, starting ⁠Deliver Nere's Head.

Quest rewards[edit section | visual editor]

  •  Bliss Spores until next long rest.
  • Access to the vine-blocked section:
    • Flumph Mating Rituals (on the dead drow)
    • Icy Metal (on the dead drow)
    • Shadow of Menzoberranzan
  • ⁠Entitled ( Noble backgrounds)

Companion approval[edit section | visual editor]

  • Reporting your success to Spaw; "The duergar are slain; the rot has been purged."  +1  +1

Notes[edit section | visual editor]

  • This can be completed without combat by lying to the Sovereign about having already killed the duergar. (Tested with having met them before talking to the Sovereign) [Bard][Deception]DC20
    • Doing this will make it impossible to start ⁠Avenge Glut's Circle
  • Avenge Glut's Circle has the same goal and brings Glut along as an additional party member (attached to the origin character, so no party slot needed).
  • Choosing "Sure, I can handle those duergar nearby." gains approval from Astarion.
