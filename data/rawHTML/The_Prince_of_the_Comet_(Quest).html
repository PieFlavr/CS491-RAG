The Prince of the Comet is a sub-quest of  Lae'zel's personal quest, ⁠The Githyanki Warrior. It is initiated in Act Three if the player character rejects an offer from ⁠Queen Vlaakith when she visits their camp.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

Early in Act Three, ⁠Vlaakith appears to the party during the Long Rest upon entering Rivington in the form of an astral projection and demands that  Lae'zel kill  Orpheus, promising to anoint her as her Chosen.

Otherwise, Lae'zel has the option to turn her down, and continue with the quest ⁠The Prince of the Comet. Depending on how previous events concerning this part of the plot unfolded earlier in the game (e.g., both learning the truth about Orpheus from  Voss and being betrayed by Vlaakith upon exiting the  Astral Prism at the Crèche Y'llek), Lae'zel may automatically refuse her demands - without any intervention by the player character.

If Lae'zel refuses, no actual consequences occur, although Vlaakith threatens that she will be hunted down by her dragon-riding warriors. The sub-quest may be resolved in the final episodes of the game, when the party gains access inside the Astral Prism, or after the fight with the  Netherbrain.

However, Lae'zel can be convinced ( DC 30 Persuasion or Wisdom Check) to change her mind again. In this case, another sub-quest ⁠Vlaakith's Will is initiated instead.
