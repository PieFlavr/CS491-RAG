Get Gortash's Netherstone

From bg3.wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Get Gortash's Netherstone is a Quest in Act Three of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be started by speaking to  Enver Gortash in the audience chamber above Wyrm's Rock Fortress.

“
Oh, goodie. Now we can go see Gortash, dangle this stone in front of him, and watch him beg before we slit his throat... right?
„
— Karlach, relishing the prospect[1]

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

After arriving in Baldur's Gate, the party needs to make their way from Rivington to Wyrm's Rock Fortress to get through to the Lower City. When the party completes ⁠Find a way into Wyrm's Rock fortress, they can enter the fortress. Once they do, a Steel Watcher instantly stops them and tells them they are intruding. However, the Watcher is taken over by the voice of Gortash, who invites them to his coronation where they can speak about the future of the Netherstones.

After entering the Audience Hall and approaching Gortash, he speaks to the party and offers an alliance, and then completes his coronation as Archduke of the city. From there, the party must decide what to do with him.

Full-on Assault[edit section | visual editor]

Once the party arrives at Wyrm's Rock Fortress, they can choose to do battle with Gortash at any point after meeting him in the Audience Hall. Gortash can be fought with before his coronation has even ended. If the Steel Watch has not been disabled, then the fight is significantly more difficult. The party can also force Gortash's hand to some extent. If they speak to him without Orin's Netherstone, he gives them one warning not to come back until they have it. Trying to speak to him again causes him to be hostile to the party.

If Gortash is defeated without his Steel Watch disabled, then Elder Brain takes over the Steel Watch and uses them to destroy the Steel Watch Foundry. It then takes control of the remaining Steel Watchers.

Disable the Steel Watch[edit section | visual editor]

Main article: Disable the Steel Watch

In order to weaken Gortash's grip on the city, the party can undertake Disable the Steel Watch. Completing this quest results in all the Steel Watchers around the city falling about, making the battle against Gortash easier. If the party goes to the Iron Throne during this quest, Gortash warns them not to go there. If they do, this signals the end of any kind of deal between him and the party. He and the entire Wyrm's Rock Fortress become hostile upon approach.

Gortash's Bargain[edit section | visual editor]

Main article: Consider Gortash's Bargain

On arriving at Wyrm's Rock Fortress, Gortash invites the party to his coronation as Archduke. If the attending and speaking with Gortash, he offers the party a pact - he will not attack them so long as they leave him and his Steel Watch alone. If the party agree to take Orin's Netherstone and return to him, he will rule the city together with them. A  DC 15 Insight Check reveals he fully intends to share his kingdom with the party.

If taking Gortash up on his offer, he asks the party to give the Netherstones to him. If agreeing, he turns on the party, as he sees them as weak-willed. If the party refuse twice, then he tells them he was simply testing them, making sure they have the backbone to make proper allies. Gortash tells the party to keep the stones and meet him at the Morphic Pool so that they can take control of the Elder Brain. However, in the confrontation with the brain, regardless of what happens, it kills Gortash, forcing the party to take the last Netherstone.

In order to ally with Gortash, the party must give up on ⁠Avenge the Ironhands, ⁠Save the Gondians, ⁠Rescue the Grand Duke, and ⁠Disable the Steel Watch. The party also should not have killed his parents in the Lower City at Flymm's Cobblers. If these actions occur, Gortash sees it as a violation of his agreement and turns hostile to the party.
