Help the Hag Survivors

From bg3.wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Help the Hag Survivors is a Quest in Act Three of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be started by interacting with an Anti-Hag Support Group Poster in the Lower City.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

Find the Hag Survivor Group[edit section | visual editor]

Upon entering the Lower City, many posters are put up around the city on various noticeboards and walls. One such noticeboard is near  Luan, the paperboy at X: 125 Y: -70. Interacting with the Anti-Hag Support Group posters begins the quest and lead to a possible meetup place at Barren's Coop near X: 45 Y: -114. The door to Barren's Coop can be opened by lockpicking or destroying it, and passing a Perception check reveals an eviction notice. To find the new location of the hag survivors, the table must be searched, revealing a barely legible letter.

The letter left at Barren's Coop reveals that the group has relocated to Old Garlow's Place at X: -49 Y: -127. As with the past location, the door can be opened by lockpicking or destroying it. Alternatively the party can go around the back and break some bookcases to enter. After entering the building, the party encounters the hag survivors, who are instantly suspicious and assume they are dealing with hagspawn.

Persuading the Group[edit section | visual editor]

Upon entering the first set of doors,  Adrielle approaches the party. Choosing the following options and successfully passing the checks has the Hag Survivors stand down:

  • [PERSUASION] A hag is after you? I can help. I've fought one before. (DC 15)
  • [INTIMIDATION] Touch me and I'll hurt you more than any bloody hag could. (DC 15)
  • [PERSUASION] We're on the same side - I'm trying to find a way to kill Auntie Ethel. (DC 15)
  • [INTIMIDATION] Touch me and I'll hurt you more than Auntie Ethel will. (DC 15)
  • [CLERIC] [PERSUASION] Wait! I'm no hagspawn - I'm a faithful adherent, just like you. (DC 20)
  • [CLERIC OF HELM] [PERSUASION] Stay your hand, Sister - I also worship He of the Watchful Eye. (DC 15 )

If the persuasion check fails, but the  Mask of Regret is saved in Act One, she stops the group from attacking the party. The Hag Survivors then explain that they are being pursued by a hag. Their leader,  Mayrina has been hexed, and they need assistance. The party can go upstairs to discover a lone sheep. If not already known, it is revealed that Mayrina has been transformed into a sheep:

  • [ANIMAL HANDLING] Examine the sheep. (DC 15)
  • [ANIMAL HANDLING] Examine Mayrina. (DC 15)

She can be spoken with using  Speak with Animals and shares that a doll caused her transformation. Reading her diary also references a doll. Clerics and Wizards have additional checks:

  • [CLERIC] [RELIGION] What could a doll have to do with a profane hex like this? (DC 15)
  • [WIZARD] [ARCANA] The magic here is wildly unorthodox - how could a doll be involved? (DC 15)

However passing the check does not offer any additional information.

Finding the Doll[edit section | visual editor]

The doll in question is the Voodoo Doll at X: -41 Y: -141, which is in the corner of the upstairs room. The doll can be attacked, injuring Mayrina in the process, or safely destroyed using  Remove Curse. Picking up the doll results in its disappearance; it does not end up in the character's inventory. Afterwards, Mayrina transforms into a human and begins shouting that the Dragonborn did it. The ruse having ended,  Jatlo reveals himself as a  Redcap servant of  Auntie Ethel. He attacks the party and summon minions to help him. Defeating Jatlo also ends the curse, turning Mayrina back into a human.

Pickpocketing Jatlo[edit section | visual editor]

If pickpocketed, Jatlo has a note using terms like "Auntie" and "hex doll." After confronting Jatlo, he transforms into a Redcap and attacks the party.

Fighting the Group[edit section | visual editor]

If the Persuasion or Deception checks fail, or if the party simply decides to fight, then the hag survivor group attacks, certain that they are facing off against hagspawn. However, after Jatlo is attacked, he transforms into a redcap and the other survivors realize they were mistaken. The survivors aid the party in taking down Jatlo and, with his defeat, Mayrina transforms into a human.

Tools to Fight the Hag[edit section | visual editor]

Mayrina tells the party that a hag is in the city and it is out to get her. She asks for help in defeating the foul creature, and states that information on how to defeat a hag is in the wall safe on the first floor at X: -62 Y: -147. The safe contains  A Hunter's Guide to Hags,  Ashes of Dried Fey Flower, and a Tearstained Diary which includes a recipe for Hag's Bane and how to use it. Agreeing to help Mayrina also sets the party on the quest ⁠Avenge the Hag Survivors.

Quest Rewards[edit section | visual editor]

  • Staff of Interruption
  • Recipe for Hag's Bane

Notes[edit section | visual editor]

  • If Auntie Ethel has already been killed, for instance while completing ⁠Save Vanra, heading to the Hag Survivors plays out differently. Mayrina is not hexed,[verify] and Jatlo has turned back into a redcap and killed himself in front of the other survivors.[verify] No Ability Checks are necessary and the quest completes after the survivors are informed of the hag's death. ⁠Avenge the Hag Survivors is skipped and the party immediately receive the  Fey Semblance Amulet, but they do not receive the Staff of Interruption.
  • Occasionally, this quest may not update when completed and a map marker may remain.
