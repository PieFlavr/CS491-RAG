The Grand Duke

From bg3.wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Grand Duke is a sub-quest of both  Wyll's ⁠Companion quest and his ⁠Origin quest. It is added to the journal after arriving at Waukeen's Rest.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

Waukeen's Rest[edit section | visual editor]

Waukeen's Rest X: -65 Y: 609 is an inn located on the Risen Road. Upon approaching, the party can smell smoke and hear shouting. At the front entrance some Flaming Fists are trying to break down the door. Upon speaking with them Gauntlet  Yeva explains that  Grand Duke Ravengard could be inside.

  • What happened to this place?
  • Grand Duke Ravengard?
  • [STRENGTH] Push against the wreckage. (DC 10)
  • [PALADIN] [STRENGTH] Let out a rallying cry as you help the Fists push. ONWARD! (DC 10 )
  • [BARD] [PERFORMANCE] WE CAN DO IT PUT YOUR BACKS INTO IT! PUSH! (DC 10)
  • There was no duke inside, just a wood elf. And she's dead. (if already witnessed deceased)

If the first two options are chosen, Yeva explains that the inn was raided by drow and goblins, and Grand Duke Ravengard was taken during the raid.

A successful Strength check smashes the door open and the Fists rush inside. The party must proceed into the room and go upstairs to find the two remaining survivors -  Counsellor Florrick and  Benryn.[2]. Attempting to rescue Benryn starts ⁠Rescue the Trapped Man.

Florrick is in the northern room X: -57 Y: 618, which the Fists swarm as they enter the building. To free her the broken door must be destroyed; she then runs out of the inn with the Fists in tow.

After rescuing her from the burning inn, Counsellor Florrick grants the party a choice between three items:

  •  The Sparky Points
  •  The Joltshooter
  •  The Spellsparkler

After learning of the Duke's kidnapping, it is also revealed that Ravengard is  Wyll's father. This starts this quest.

Goblin Camp[edit section | visual editor]

At the Goblin Camp, the party can learn more of Duke Ravengard's fate. If toasting with the goblins after poisoning the booze tub X: -99 Y: 442,  Warlock Gurk says as part of the toast: To tha' duke's face when we gave 'im to the drow!"

Last Light Inn[edit section | visual editor]

During Act Two, the party can encounter Counsellor Florrick at the Last Light Inn with an entourage of Flaming Fists. She asks if the party have made any inroads with Moonrise Towers. Regardless of the response, she implores the party to keep looking. She herself, however, has to depart for Baldur's Gate to seek reinforcements.

Moonrise Towers[edit section | visual editor]

In the Moonrise Towers Prison, the party can ask the  Warden if she has seen the Duke. However, she responds that he sounds important and she only deals with the dregs down in the prison. The party can also interrogate the corpse of a dead goblin scout,  Sharp-Eye Lora, who reveals that she took prisoners to Moonrise Towers, including a duke.

Mind Flayer Colony[edit section | visual editor]

The Duke can be found near the end of Act Two in the Mind Flayer Colony, while chasing after  Ketheric Thorm as part of ⁠Defeat Ketheric Thorm, the party encounter the Chosen Three - Thorm,  Orin the Red, and  Enver Gortash, and with them they have Ulder Ravengard. The party can watch in the shadows as the trio infect Ravengard with a mind flayer tadpole and then whisk him away to Baldur's Gate.

Wyrm's Rock Fortress[edit section | visual editor]

In Act Three, Duke Ravengard can be encountered once again after completing ⁠Find a way into Wyrm's Rock fortress. Once entering Wyrm's Rock Fortress, Gortash takes control of a Steel Watcher and invites the party to his coronation. Ravengard is in the Audience Chamber with Gortash and officiates Gortash's swearing in as Archduke of Baldur's Gate. Immediately after the ceremony, Ravengard can be spoken with. If saying that Florrick sent the party, he replies that Florrick is a black widow who betrayed the city, and was punished. Choosing to connect with Ravengard's parasite to seek information about Florrick reveals that she is imprisoned in Wyrm's Rock Prison and set to be executed, starting ⁠Free Counselor Florrick. If the party prod Ravengard's mind further, they find deep uncertainty and fear of an illithid invasion within him, indicating that his mind has not fully been taken over.

Once the coronation is complete,  Mizora appears and reveals that Duke Ravengard is already being moved to another location to be disposed of. She refuses to elaborate and tells the party to go to camp. After a long rest, Mizora appears before Wyll to present a new contract - an eternity of servitude to  Zariel in exchange for rescuing the Duke. If Wyll signs the contract, she reveals that Duke Ravengard is in Gortash's underwater prison, the Iron Throne and promises to keep him from harm. Otherwise, if Wyll refuses to sign the new contract, Mizora states that he is as good as dead, but she plans to stick around to see what happens. Even if Wyll does not sign the contract with Mizora, Duke Ravengard can still be rescued, if completing either ⁠Disable the Steel Watch, ⁠Save the Gondians, or ⁠Avenge the Ironhands.

If ignoring the coronation and moving into the Lower City, then Duke Ravengard remains in the Audience Hall with Gortash until the ceremony is completed. If completing the questline ⁠Disable the Steel Watch without attending the coronation, then the entirety of Wyrm's Rock Fortress becomes hostile, including Duke Ravengard. To ensure that the Duke can be rescued, it is advised to attend the coronation, speak to Mizora, and then take a long rest.

The Iron Throne[edit section | visual editor]

Duke Ravengard is in the block of cells directly east from the ladder to the submersible. He is in the middle cell, with only 35 HP. He also has the  Fiendish Obeisance condition, unless Wyll is not recruited to the party. Upon opening the door to his cell, the Duke becomes an Attached Follower. However, once he leaves the cell, if Wyll broke his pact with Mizora, she appears and summons multiple  Combustion Belly Spiderlings to kill the Duke. She orders him to kneel while the spiderlings explode on their turn. The Duke can be saved by healing him while he is in his cell via  Healing Word, throwing healing potions, or with spells such as  Arcane Gate.

If Duke Ravengard is saved, he stays at camp under the protection of the  Astral Prism. If Wyll is recruited to the party, the pair can reconcile, and Ravengard asks the party to seek out  Ansur, Baldur's Gate's guardian dragon who sleeps under the city.

Quest Rewards[edit section | visual editor]

  • If Duke Ravengard is rescued, he becomes an ally as part of ⁠Gather Your Allies.
