Lift the Shadow Curse

From bg3.wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Lift the Shadow Curse is a Quest in Act Two of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be started by speaking with  Halsin after entering the Shadow-Cursed Lands.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

This quest can only be undertaken if ⁠Defeat the Goblins is completed and  Halsin agrees to accompany the party to Moonrise Towers.

It can be initiated by speaking with Halsin while at the Campsite and asking him how to lift the Shadow Curse. He explains that the land is not made up merely of soil and roots, but has a spirit of its own - a young fey boy named  Thaniel. Halsin has not felt the boy's presence since the curse was unleashed, and believes he was banished to the Shadowfell. Halsin asks that the party look out for any clues as they venture through the Shadow-Cursed Lands.

The quest can also be started if having  Art Cullagh's Battered Lute in the inventory of the talking party member when interacting with Art while Halsin is in camp.

The Spirit of the Land[edit section | visual editor]

Information regarding Thaniel's whereabouts can be found in the Last Light Inn, in a room on the northern end of the ground floor. Inside, a man named  Art Cullagh is lying on one of the beds, unresponsive and singing a song about climbing trees with Thaniel - whose name he could only know had he actually met the boy himself.

Interacting with him starts the quest ⁠Wake Art Cullagh. To proceed, the party must obtain the  Battered Lute from  Malus Thorm within the House of Healing and bring it back to Cullagh in order to wake him up. Once awake, Cullagh asks to see Halsin, as Thaniel had told him to seek the druid out.

Halsin may already be at the inn if told about Art's condition beforehand, or the party can go fetch him now. Either way Halsin asks Cullagh what he remembers of Thaniel and where they met. Though Cullagh struggles to explain the shifting landscape of the Shadowfell, he does remember that he always smelled lavender when Thaniel was around.

Alternatively, if Art Cullagh dies due to  Isobel being kidnapped or is killed during ⁠Resolve the Abduction, then  Speak with Dead can be used to get the same information from his corpse.

Once given this information, Halsin asks the party to follow him down to the lakeside, which is north of the Last Light Inn, just outside of Isobel's barrier. Once there, speaking with Halsin results in him explaining that he has spent years studying and seeking the Oak Father's favour in order to find a way into the Shadowfell. However, he needs the party's help to keep the portal open so that he can return, since once he enters, those taken by the shadow curse will swarm the portal in an attempt to destroy it.

After Halsin has entered the portal, waves of enemies begin to spawn. They prioritise targeting the portal to the Shadowfell above all else. The portal must be kept intact for four turns, at which point Halsin returns. The enemies which spawn include  Wraiths,  Shadows,  Shadow Mastiffs,  Shadow Creepers, and Shadow-Cursed members of the Harpers, the Flaming Fist, and the Cult of the Absolute, as well as some Shadow-Cursed Githyanki Warriors. If the portal is destroyed, Halsin is lost to the Shadowfell forever, and the quest fails.

  • Wave 1: Shadow ×3, Wraith ×1, Shadow Mastiff ×2, Shadow-Cursed Harper ×2, Shadow-Cursed Pilgrim ×3
  • Wave 2: Shadow-Cursed Raven ×8, Shadow Creeper ×3, Shadow-Cursed Pilgrim ×2, Shadow-Cursed Harper ×2, Shadow-Cursed Flaming Fist ×3
  • Wave 3: Shadow ×1, Wraith ×1, Shadow-Cursed Zealot ×3, Shadow-Cursed Flaming Fist ×2, Shadow-Cursed Githyanki Warrior ×3

If Halsin succeeds, he exits the portal with an unconscious Thaniel in his arms. He explains that something is wrong, as it seems a part of Thaniel is missing, and asks the party to meet him back at camp to discuss what to do next. Party members with the  Outlander background gain the ⁠Grove Keeper's Keeper inspiration for defending the portal.

At camp, Halsin reveals that Thaniel's spirit was split in two when the curse took hold - half was taken to the Shadowfell, but half of him was left behind amidst the curse, and while that half may have been strong enough to resist, it has surely succumbed to the curse over the past hundred years. If it can be found, however, it can be reunited with Thaniel, allowing the land to finally heal.

While seeming like a "needle in a haystack" situation at first, Halsin explains that the curse cannot completely suppress Thaniel's power, and that the party should look for signs of life in the darkness such as wildflowers where everything else has died. He points the party towards the Ruined Battlefield and a house where he saw several fresh blooms.

At this point, Halsin joins the party as a full Companion. It is recommended to make room in the current party for him to come along for the next stage of the quest.

Oliver[edit section | visual editor]

 Oliver is the missing half of Thaniel's spirit. He is located in the House in Deep Shadows (which is marked on the map as "House of Flowers"), found to the east of Last Light Inn. If the party has not already played hide-and-seek with him, he might ask them to do so - but regardless of whether or not his game is played, Oliver gets angry when reminded of who Thaniel is, and eventually runs away into a shadow portal.

Use the portal to follow Oliver to the middle of Reithwin Town. Oliver shields himself under a Nightdome, which reflects any damage it is dealt back to the attacker. He then summons several enemies to protect him: two Wraiths called  Mummy and  Daddy, as well as a shadowy owlbear called  Shadow Plush. He also summons five Shadow Friends at a time. Killing each of Oliver's summoned allies deals damage to the Nightdome without reflecting the damage back.

Once the dome is destroyed, party members with the  Entertainer background gain the ⁠It Came From Shadows inspiration. Oliver complains about being disturbed and says he does not want to go back to Thaniel, who he perceives as having abandoned him. The party can choose to be kind when convincing Oliver to rejoin Thaniel and be whole again, or be aggressive; they can also let Halsin handle it. Oliver always agrees to rejoin with Thaniel, but being aggressive about it causes Halsin to lose approval, while allowing him to handle it or being kind gives a large boost to his approval.

Healing the Shadow-Cursed Lands[edit section | visual editor]

After Oliver finally returns to Thaniel, the boy finally awakens. He appears at camp, near Halsin's tent. He explains that even though he is awake, the land cannot truly heal until  Ketheric Thorm - the one responsible for originally cursing it - is dead, once and for all. The quest is not fully resolved until ⁠Defeat Ketheric Thorm is completed and the party leave the Shadow-Cursed Lands to venture forth to Baldur's Gate, at which point a cutscene triggers showing the land beginning to heal.

If this quest is not resolved when leaving the Shadow-Cursed Lands, Halsin leaves the party and chooses to remain behind to look after the land and attempt to lift the Shadow Curse himself.
