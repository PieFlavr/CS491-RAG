Rescue the Grand Duke

From bg3.wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Rescue the Grand Duke is a quest in Act One of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be started by approaching the burning inn on The Risen Road, Waukeen's Rest.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

Waukeen's Rest[edit section | visual editor]

Warning! Approaching Waukeen's Rest closely enough to trigger companion dialogue and then long resting, fast traveling (e.g. to camp) or venturing to Rosymorn Monastery Trail, causes the trapped survivors, including Florrick, to die. If this happens, this quest can still be obtained in Act Three.

Waukeen's Rest is an inn located on the Risen Road at X: -65 Y: 609. As the party approach, they smell smoke and hear shouting.

Through the front door[edit section | visual editor]

At the front entrance there is a group of Flaming Fists trying to bust down the door. Gauntlet  Yeva explains that  Grand Duke Ravengard could be in there.

  • What happened to this place?
  • Grand Duke Ravengard?
  • [STRENGTH] Push against the wreckage. (DC 10)
  • [PALADIN] [STRENGTH] Let out a rallying cry as you help the Fists push. ONWARD! (DC 10 )
  • [BARD] [PERFORMANCE] WE CAN DO IT! PUT YOUR BACKS INTO IT! PUSH! (DC 10)
  • You heard her, Wyll. Get in there. (auto-success if Wyll is present)

If the first two options are chosen, Yeva explains that the inn was raided by drow and goblins. Grand Duke Ravengard was lost during the raid.

A successful Strength check smashes the door open and the Fists rush inside. The party must go straight into the room to find a set of stairs, and climb it to find the two remaining survivors  Counsellor Florrick and  Benryn. Attempting to rescue Benryn starts ⁠Rescue the Trapped Man. Following the orders to go into the burning inn also grants the ⁠Chain of Command inspiration to party members with the Soldier background.

Florrick is in the northern room at X: -57 Y: 618, which the Fists swarm as they enter the building. Attacking and destroying the broken door frees her. She then runs out of the inn with the Fists in tow, granting the following inspiration:

  • ⁠Rescued from the Flames ( Folk Hero backgrounds)
  • ⁠Protector of the Duke ( Noble backgrounds)

Through the south-eastern building[edit section | visual editor]

In the south-eastern building there's a room filled with crates and barrels. This room has a broken ceiling. Using  Misty Step,  Fly or utilizing  Jump with a combination of stapled crates gets the party on the first floor. The party must head through the door, which has to be smashed or lockpicked. Behind the door is a small bridge that leads to another door that also needs to be smashed or lockpicked. Behind this door is the room where Florrick is found. Approaching her makes her leave the room, head to the bridge and through the second door. She then jumps or climbs to the ground floor and runs to the Flaming Fists at the front door.

If Florrick is saved[edit section | visual editor]

Outside, she requests that the party rescue the Duke, then leave to organize her own efforts. Before she leaves, however, she gives the party three rewards to choose from:

  • The Joltshooter
  • The Sparky Points
  • The Spellsparkler

After learning of the Duke's kidnapping, it is also revealed that Ravengard is Wyll's father. This starts part of Wyll's companion quest, ⁠The Grand Duke.

By casting  Speak with Dead on a Dead Drow Raider near the entrance of Waukeen's Rest, the party can learn that the Cult of the Absolute is related to the kidnapping, and that the Grand Duke might have been taken to Moonrise Towers.

At the Goblin Camp, the party can learn slightly more. If the party do a toast after poisoning the booze tub at X: -99 Y: 442. As part of the toast,  Warlock Gurk says "To tha' duke's face when we gave 'im to the drow!"

If Florrick dies[edit section | visual editor]

If Florrick dies because the party did not rescue her in time, later events in Act Two and Act Three with Florrick will not occur. However, Rescue the Grand Duke can still be obtained in Act Three at Wyrm's Rock Fortress.

Last Light Inn[edit section | visual editor]

During Act Two, the party can encounter Counsellor Florrick at the Last Light Inn with an entourage of Flaming Fists. She asks if the party have made any inroads with Moonrise Towers. Regardless of the response, she implores the party to keep looking. Florrick, however, departs for Baldur's Gate to seek reinforcements.

Moonrise Towers[edit section | visual editor]

In Moonrise Towers Prison, the party can ask the  Warden if she has seen the Duke. However, she responds that he sounds important and that she only deals with the dregs down in the prison. The party can also interrogate the corpse of a dead goblin scout,  Sharp-Eye Lora, who reveals that she took prisoners to Moonrise Towers including a duke.

The Duke can eventually be found near the end of Act Two, during ⁠Defeat Ketheric Thorm. In the Mind Flayer Colony, as the party chase after ⁠Ketheric Thorm, they encounter the Chosen Three - Thorm,  Orin the Red, and  Enver Gortash, and with them Ulder Ravengard. The party watch in the shadows as the trio infect Ravengard with a mind flayer tadpole and then whisk him away to Baldur's Gate.

Wyrm's Rock Fortress[edit section | visual editor]

In Act Three, Duke Ravengard is encountered once again after completing ⁠Find a way into Wyrm's Rock fortress. Once the party enter Wyrm's Rock Fortress, Gortash takes control of a Steel Watcher and invites the party to his coronation. Ravengard is in the Audience Chamber with Gortash and officiates Gortash's swearing in as Archduke of Baldur's Gate. Immediately after the ceremony, Ravengard can be spoken to.

Councilor Florrick[edit section | visual editor]

If Florrick was rescued at Waukeen's Rest and the party mention that she sent them, he says that she is a black widow who betrayed the city, and that she was punished. Choosing to connect with Ravengard's parasite to seek information about Florrick reveals that she is imprisoned in Wyrm's Rock Prison and set to be executed, starting ⁠Free Counselor Florrick. If Florrick died at Waukeen's Rest the ⁠Free Counselor Florrick quest cannot be obtained. The party can prod Ravengard's mind further, and find out that deep uncertainty and fear of an illithid invasion is within him, indicating his mind has not fully been taken over.

After the coronation[edit section | visual editor]

Once the coronation is complete,  Mizora appears and reveals that Duke Ravengard is already being moved to another location to be disposed of. She refuses to elaborate further and tells the party to go to camp. After a Long Rest, Mizora appears before Wyll to present a new contract - an eternity of servitude for Zariel in exchange for rescuing the Duke. If Wyll signs the contract, she reveals that Duke Ravengard is in Gortash's underwater prison, the Iron Throne and promises to keep him from harm. Otherwise, if Wyll breaks the contract, Mizora states that he is as good as dead, but that she plans to stick around to see what happens.

If the party ignore the coronation and move into the Lower City, then Duke Ravengard remains in the Audience Hall with Gortash until the ceremony is completed. If the party complete the ⁠Disable the Steel Watch without attending the coronation, then the entirety of Wyrm's Rock Fortress becomes hostile, including Duke Ravengard. To ensure that the Duke can be rescued, it is advised to attend the coronation, speak to Mizora, and take a long rest to ensure that he can be found in his intended location.

The Iron Throne[edit section | visual editor]

After the previous events, Duke Ravengard is moved to the Iron Throne, either alive or dead. The party may find the Iron Throne either by exploring the Lower City, or be led there as part of the quests ⁠Save the Gondians, or ⁠Avenge the Ironhands.

Duke Ravengard is in the block of cells directly east from the ladder to the submersible. Normally, he is in the middle cell, alive, and with only 35 HP. He also has the  Fiendish Obeisance status, unless Wyll was not recruited. Upon opening the door to his cell, the party gains control over the Duke's movements. However, once he leaves the cell, if Wyll broke his pact with Mizora, then she appears and summons multiple Combustion Belly Spiderlings to kill the duke. She orders him to kneel while the spiderlings explode on their turn. The Duke can be saved by healing him while he is in his cell (via  Healing Word, throwing healing potions, or other tactics) or through spells like  Arcane Gate.

Under some circumstances, the duke is found already dead. After escaping the Iron Throne, the party may be able to make a deal with Mizora to resurrect the duke in exchange for renewing Wyll's pact, or reject the deal in order to break his pact. The availability of this option may depend on Wyll's status and earlier choices.

If Duke Ravengard is saved, he stays at camp under the protection of the  Astral Prism. If Wyll was recruited, the pair can reconcile, and Ravengard asks the party to seek out  Ansur, Baldur's Gate's guardian dragon who sleeps under the city.

If Gortash is defeated before traveling to the Iron Throne, the prison is automatically destroyed and Duke Ravengard is killed, although only implicitly. But if Wyll made the pact with Mizora, she brings him back to life in camp as if nothing happened.

Quest rewards[edit section | visual editor]

After rescuing her from the burning inn, Counsellor Florrick grants the party a choice between three items, which all have similar abilities:

  • The Joltshooter
  • The Sparky Points
  • The Spellsparkler
  • If Duke Ravengard is rescued, he becomes an ally as part of ⁠Gather Your Allies.

Bugs[edit section | visual editor]

Even if Wyll decides to break Mizora's Pact and allows his father to die for his freedom, Karlach may, if spoken with immediately after Mizora's cut-scene concludes, still comment as if Wyll signed his soul over eternally to Zariel.[verify]
