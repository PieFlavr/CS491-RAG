Free Counsellor Florrick

From bg3.wiki

Free Counsellor Florrick is a Quest in Act Three of Baldur's Gate 3. It can be started by reading the execution notice near the gallows of the Lower City. It is a time-sensitive quest that must be completed within five long rests.

Walkthrough[edit section | visual editor]

The quest to free Counsellor Florrick begins after interacting with an Execution Notice, of which copies are posted throughout the Lower City, or after speaking to  Ulder Ravengard right after the coronation. These notices state that  Counsellor Florrick will be executed within five days. This starts a timer - Florrick dies, if she is not saved within five long rests. If too many rests have occured, then the quest automatically updates, as Florrick has been executed. The quest can also be started by speaking to Ulder Ravengard in camp after rescuing him, which does not initiate the timer.

While the quest is in progress, the Execution Notices change their text, which helps to count down the number of long rests left.

Counsellor Florrick is kept in the Wyrm's Rock Prison. There are only two penitentiaries within the Act Three locations, and thus, the party may visit both to find Florrick. Otherwise, she can be located by talking to  Fist 'Edge' Arensen near the gallows in Lower City, or by reading the  Prison Log in the Wyrm's Rock Prison (if the quest is initiated, the log contains an entry about Florrick).

Walking through the front door[edit section | visual editor]

The entrance to Wyrm's Rock Prison is within Wyrm's Rock Fortress, down a passageway in the southwest room at X: -25 Y: 210. The door to the prison is guarded by a sleeping Flaming Fist,  Breva Brightmoon. With a successful perception check, the party can listen in to what she is sleep-talking about and find out there is a man named  Otto Ott in prison. If waking Breva up, the party can convince her to let them pass:

  • I'm here to see Old Man Otto. He's my father.
  • [PERSUASION] Duke Ravengard has sent me to interrogate Florrick. (DC 15)
  • [PERSUASION] I'm here for Arfur. It's time he answered my questions. (DC 15)
  • [INTIMIDATION] Prison inspection. Open the door and pray I don't find more sleeping guards. (DC 15)
  • I don't have business here. I'm just exploring.
  • [DETECT THOUGHTS] [WISDOM] Read the guard's mind.
    • You let me through, and I won't have to tell the manip of your lunchtime tipples.

Choosing the first three options allows the party to enter the jail without the guards becoming hostile, at least until Florrick is released from her cell.

Letting Breva sleep and stealing the prison key from her also gets the party in.

Sneaking into Wyrm's Rock[edit section | visual editor]

Wyrm's Rock Prison can easily be infiltrated via a hole in the foundation - accessed through Rivington. This is accessed by jumping off using  Fly or  Feather Fall at the eastern side of the gate to Wyrm's Rock at X: 27 Y: 158. After this, walking eastward reveals a crack in the wall to the prison at X: 42 Y: 190 with a successful perception check.

After entering into Wyrm's Rock Prison, there is a wall that can be blasted open at X: 91 Y: -720 with blunt attacks or spells (e.g  Eldritch Blast). This leads to a storage room. The door must be lockpicked open. There are multiple guards that confront the party if they are spotted - requiring either successful sneaking or passing a  DC 15 Deception Check.

Exiting the storeroom, there is only one Flaming Fist guarding the way forward -  Fist Ivarus. Moving past him and going northward, the party can approach the prisoner's cells. There is a small alcove with a table where three more Fists sit ( Fist Jagic,  Fist Saunder, and  Fist Creekrider). Counsellor Florrick is in the northwestern cell. There are no other prisoners to liberate (the only other prisoner is Otto Ott, who complains about being let out of his cell and refuses to leave). Florrick's cell can be lockpicked with a  DC 15 Sleight of Hand Check.

Freeing Florrick[edit section | visual editor]

When spoken with, Florrick is initially demoralized and needs to be convinced to escape:

  • Ravengard's in a safe place, Florrick. All is not lost. (if Ravengard already rescued)
  • [PERSUASION] Ravengard's dead. The city needs you - I'm getting you out. (DC 10)
  • [PERSUASION] I'm breaking you out. With Ravengard gone, Baldur's Gate needs you. (DC 10)
  • [PERSUASION] I'm breaking you out. Baldur's Gate needs you. Ravengard needs you. (DC 10)
  • [PERSUASION] You said you wouldn't give up. I'm setting you free and holding you to it. (DC 7)
  • [INTIMIDATION] Pathetic. I'm getting you out so I can beat some sense into you. (DC 10)
  • Ravengard's not lost. I felt him reach out to me.

Failing a check results in a second chance:

  • [PERSUASION] I pull you from flames, and this is how you repay me? Find your courage and fight! (DC 10)
  • [BALDURIAN] [HISTORY] Coward. Balduran didn't build the Gate for you to weep as it burns. (DC 7)
  • [HISTORY] Gorion's Ward overcame great odds to protect the ciy. You can do the same. (DC 10) (passive History check passed))

Passing a check allows to convince Florrick to break out. Regardless of the route used to enter Wyrm's Rock, the hole in the wall in the storeroom can be used to easily escape. It is possible to apply  Invisibility on Florrick, as the guards only turn hostile if she is spotted. Otherwise, using  Misty Step or  Dimension Door can also bring Florrick out of the cell, and the guards do not pursue her once she has left through the crack in the wall. Florrick also has a number of excellent crowd control spells at her disposal, including  Hypnotic Pattern.

Once having escaped Wyrm's Rock Prison, the party must speak to Florrick, who thanks them before departing to the Upper City to seek allies.

Despite the entrance to The Wyrmway being in this location, it is not possible to keep Florrick in the party while running the related quest; she proceeds through the puzzle entrance but refuses at the first door after it ("Now is not the time for exploring"). She can, however, be left waiting at that location.

Quest rewards[edit section | visual editor]

  • If Florrick is freed, then she is added to ⁠Gather Your Allies as an ally, granting  Florrick's Cohort during the game's final fight.
  • ⁠Diplomatic Rescue ( Folk Hero background)
  • ⁠Wrongly Convicted ( Criminal background)
